Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

1. Introduction

This Privacy Policy explains how ChronoCode ("we", "us", "our") collects, uses, stores, and protects your personal information when you use our website, desktop application, and related services. We are committed to protecting your privacy and being transparent about our data practices.

By using ChronoCode, you agree to the collection and use of information in accordance with this policy. If you do not agree, please discontinue use of the Service.

2. Information We Collect

We collect information in the following categories:

  • Account Information: When you sign in, we collect your email address, display name, username, and profile picture provided by your authentication provider (Google OAuth or sandbox email).
  • Usage Data: We collect file snapshots metadata, IDE activity logs, extension usage patterns, and feature interaction data to improve the Service.
  • Device Information: We collect your operating system, browser type and version, IP address, and device identifiers for analytics and security purposes.

File snapshot content is processed locally on your machine. Only metadata (file paths, timestamps, change sizes) is transmitted to our servers.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • Provide the Service: To deliver core ChronoCode features including snapshot management, timeline tracking, and real-time telemetry.
  • Improve the Product: To understand how users interact with ChronoCode, identify bugs, and develop new features.
  • Communicate: To send you service updates, security alerts, and respond to your support requests.
  • Security: To detect and prevent fraud, abuse, and unauthorized access to our systems.

4. Authentication

ChronoCode supports sign-in via Google OAuth and sandbox email authentication. When you use Google OAuth, we receive only your email address and display name. We do not access your Google contacts, calendar, or any other Google services.

For sandbox email authentication, we store only the email address and name you provide during registration. We do not store passwords for sandbox accounts.

5. Cookies & Tracking

We use cookies and similar technologies for the following purposes:

  • Session Cookies: To maintain your logged-in state and provide a seamless experience across pages.
  • Analytics Cookies: To understand how visitors interact with our website, including pages viewed and features used.
  • Preference Cookies: To remember your settings such as theme preferences and language selection.

For full details on managing cookies, see our Cookie Policy.

6. Data Storage

Your data is stored using the following infrastructure:

  • Encryption at Rest: All data stored on our servers is encrypted at rest using industry-standard AES-256 encryption.
  • Vercel Infrastructure: Our website and API are hosted on Vercel's secure cloud infrastructure with built-in DDoS protection.
  • MongoDB Atlas: User data and metadata are stored in MongoDB Atlas with encrypted connections and access controls.

7. Data Retention

We retain your account data for as long as your account is active. When you delete your account, we remove your personal data within 30 days.

File snapshot metadata is retained according to your retention policy settings. You can configure retention periods in the ChronoCode settings to automatically manage old snapshots.

8. Third-Party Services

We use the following third-party services that may process your data:

  • Google OAuth: For authentication. Governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Vercel: For hosting and infrastructure. Governed by Vercel's Privacy Policy.
  • MongoDB Atlas: For database storage. Governed by MongoDB's Privacy Policy.

9. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal data to third parties. We may share your information only in the following circumstances:

  • With your explicit consent.
  • To comply with applicable laws, regulations, or valid legal processes.
  • To protect the rights, property, or safety of ChronoCode, our users, or the public.
  • In connection with a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, with notice to affected users.

10. Data Security

We implement multiple layers of security to protect your data:

  • Encryption: All data in transit is encrypted using TLS 1.3. Data at rest is encrypted with AES-256.
  • Access Controls: Strict role-based access controls ensure only authorized personnel can access user data.
  • Regular Audits: We conduct regular security audits and penetration testing to identify and address vulnerabilities.

While we take all reasonable measures, no method of transmission or storage is 100% secure. We cannot guarantee absolute security.

11. Your Rights

You have the following rights regarding your personal data:

  • Access: Request a copy of the personal data we hold about you.
  • Correct: Request correction of inaccurate or incomplete data.
  • Delete: Request deletion of your personal data and account.
  • Export: Request a portable copy of your data in a structured format.
  • Opt-out: Opt out of non-essential data collection and marketing communications.

To exercise any of these rights, contact us at privacy@chronocode.app.

12. Children's Privacy

ChronoCode is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that we have collected data from a child under 13, we will take steps to delete that information promptly.

13. International Data Transfers

ChronoCode operates primarily from India. If you access the Service from outside India, your data may be transferred to and processed in India. By using the Service, you consent to such transfers. We ensure that appropriate safeguards are in place to protect your data during international transfers.

14. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. For material changes, we will provide at least 30 days' notice before the changes take effect through email notification or a prominent notice on our website.

15. Contact

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us:
