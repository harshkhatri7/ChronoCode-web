Refund Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

1. Overview

The ChronoCode Pro License is a one-time payment with no recurring charges or subscriptions. We want you to be satisfied with your purchase. If you're not happy, we offer refunds under the conditions described in this policy.

2. Eligibility

You may be eligible for a refund if:

  • The refund request is made within 14 days of your original purchase date.
  • You have not extensively used the Pro features after purchase (e.g., activated large projects, used API-heavy features extensively).
  • The Pro activation key has not been shared with others.

Refund eligibility is evaluated on a case-by-case basis. We reserve the right to deny refund requests that don't meet these criteria.

3. How to Request a Refund

To request a refund, please follow these steps:

  • Send a Direct Message (DM) to our Instagram account: @chronocode.ai
  • Include your email address used for the purchase.
  • Attach proof of purchase (screenshot of the WhatsApp transaction or payment confirmation).
  • Briefly describe the reason for your refund request (optional but helpful).

Our team will review your request and respond within 48 hours.

4. Processing Time

Once your refund is approved, the refund will be processed within 5-7 business days. The refund will be issued to the original payment method used during the purchase.

Please allow additional time for the refund to appear in your account, as processing times may vary depending on your payment provider or bank.

5. Exceptions

Refunds may be denied in the following circumstances:

  • The Pro features have been heavily used after purchase (extensive snapshot activity, API calls, large project integration).
  • The activation key has been shared or redistributed to other users or devices.
  • The refund request is made after 14 days from the purchase date.
  • The refund request is made in bad faith or appears to be an attempt to abuse the refund policy.

6. Chargebacks

If you initiate a chargeback or payment dispute without contacting us first, we reserve the right to:

  • Immediately deactivate your Pro license.
  • Block future purchases from your account.

We strongly encourage you to contact us first. We are committed to resolving any issues quickly and fairly. Most issues can be resolved without the need for a chargeback.

7. Free Tier

The ChronoCode free tier is always free with no hidden charges. There is no refund needed for the free tier as no payment is required. The free tier provides access to core versioning features with certain limits.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this Refund Policy or need assistance with your refund request, please reach out to us:

  • Instagram: @chronocode.ai
  • Email: support@chronocode.app
