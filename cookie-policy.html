Cookie Policy

Last updated: June 25, 2026

1. What Are Cookies

Cookies are small text files that are stored on your device when you visit a website. They are widely used to make websites work efficiently and to provide information to website owners. Cookies help us understand how you use ChronoCode and improve your experience.

2. Essential Cookies

These cookies are necessary for the basic functionality of ChronoCode. They enable core features like authentication and security.

  • Session Cookie: Maintains your logged-in state as you navigate across pages. Expires when you close your browser.
  • Auth Token: Stores your authentication credentials securely to keep you signed in. Expires after 30 days.
  • CSRF Token: Protects against cross-site request forgery attacks. Expires with your session.

Essential cookies cannot be disabled as they are required for the Service to function properly.

3. Analytics Cookies

Analytics cookies help us understand how visitors interact with our website. They collect information about:

  • Pages visited and time spent on each page.
  • Features used and navigation patterns.
  • Referring websites and traffic sources.
  • Device type, browser, and screen resolution.

This data is aggregated and anonymized, meaning it cannot be used to identify you personally. It helps us improve the website experience for all users.

4. Preference Cookies

Preference cookies allow the website to remember choices you make to provide personalized features:

  • Theme Preference: Remembers whether you prefer dark or light mode.
  • Language Selection: Stores your preferred language for the interface.
  • Layout Preferences: Remembers your dashboard layout and panel configurations.

5. Third-Party Cookies

We may use the following third-party services that set their own cookies:

  • Google Analytics: Helps us analyze website traffic and usage patterns. Google Analytics cookies collect information anonymously. You can opt out using the Google Analytics Opt-out Browser Add-on.

Third-party cookies are subject to the respective privacy policies of those services.

6. Managing Cookies

You can control and manage cookies through your browser settings. Here are instructions for popular browsers:

  • Chrome: Go to Settings > Privacy and Security > Cookies and other site data. You can block all cookies or allow only specific sites.
  • Firefox: Go to Settings > Privacy & Security. Under Enhanced Tracking Protection, choose Standard, Strict, or Custom to manage cookie preferences.
  • Safari: Go to Preferences > Privacy. Select "Manage Website Data" to view and remove cookies, or enable "Prevent cross-site tracking."
  • Edge: Go to Settings > Cookies and site permissions > Manage and delete cookies. Choose to block, allow, or clear cookies on exit.

7. Impact of Disabling Cookies

If you disable or block cookies, some features of ChronoCode may not function correctly:

  • You may be unable to log in or your session may expire prematurely.
  • Your preferences (theme, language) may not be remembered.
  • Website performance and analytics may be affected.

Essential cookies are required for the Service to function. Disabling them may render the website unusable.

8. Cookie Consent

When you first visit ChronoCode, you will be presented with a cookie consent banner. You can choose to accept all cookies, reject non-essential cookies, or customize your preferences. You can change your cookie preferences at any time through the website settings.

By continuing to use the website after the consent banner, you agree to our use of cookies as described in this policy.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Cookie Policy from time to time to reflect changes in our practices or applicable laws. For material changes, we will provide at least 30 days' notice before the changes take effect.
